About

My Story

I am transitioning to UX/UI design with a background in technical support & customer service. In my line of work, I have had to think outside of the box to find solutions to various user issues and meet business needs.

During my college years, I was introduced to design when I worked as an IT Support Technician. I worked alongside our in-house graphic designer and web developer to create our IT support webpage.

This inspired me to pursue web development courses and enroll in my first UI design course. I was immediately captivated by the idea of solving problems through creative means. Therefore, I took a leap of faith and officially began my UX/UI journey with CareerFoundry. As a UX/UI designer, I aspire to create unique user experiences and alluring app interfaces.
